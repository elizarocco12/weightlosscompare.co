Oral GLP-1

Rybelsus (Oral Semaglutide)

The first oral GLP-1 medication approved for weight management in the UK. Same active ingredient as Wegovy injection but taken as a daily tablet. Available from online doctors from ~£149/month.

From £149/month
Prescription

Orlistat (Prescription)

Blocks ~30% of dietary fat absorption. Available on NHS prescription or privately. Must be combined with a reduced-calorie diet to be effective. Most cost-effective prescription option available.

From £17.49/month
OTC

Alli (OTC Orlistat 60mg)

Lower-dose version of orlistat available without prescription from pharmacies. Reduces fat absorption, must be combined with a calorie-controlled low-fat diet. Half the prescription dose.

From £29.99/month
Prescription

Mysimba (Naltrexone/Bupropion)

Combination pill that targets the brain's reward system to reduce appetite and food cravings. NICE-approved for adults with BMI 30+ or 27+ with weight-related conditions.

From £89/month
